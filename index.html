Who We Are

An India-Based Global Trading Company

AAAMS Global Ventures connects manufacturers, suppliers, distributors, and buyers across international markets. We operate across Export, Import, Global Sourcing, and E-Commerce — making cross-border trade simpler, faster, and more reliable.

Based in Delhi, India, we serve buyers and businesses in Canada, the USA, the United Kingdom, Europe, and the Middle East — with a focus on quality, transparency, and long-term relationships.

Our Mission
To connect global buyers and suppliers through transparent, reliable, and efficient trade solutions.
Our Vision
